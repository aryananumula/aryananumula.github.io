Early detection of cavities, gum disease, and more personalized recommendations for preventative care. Convenient and easy-to-use at home. Data-driven insights for you and your dentist.

The goal of this AI project is to provide oral health diagnosis to poor people who can’t afford to go to a dentist, particularly in countries where there is no universal health coverage. The smart probe is small, easy to use, and can easily be integrated with your current oral care routine. It will take pictures of your mouth and use AI to analyze your brushing habits and provide you with personalized feedback on your oral health. The device uses cutting-edge imaging technology to take detailed pictures of your oral environment. This allows you to analyze your dental surfaces, the health of your gum tissue, and the distribution of plaque. By integrating AI algorithms, you can assess your brushing techniques in real-time, identify areas of improvement, and provide personalized recommendations to improve your oral hygiene practice.

Project Goal is: The goal is to design an AI-powered system that makes dental health analysis and preliminary diagnosis more accessible to rural communities who live far away from dental service providers, quicker than going to a dental checkup and cheaper to people than in-person dental screening service. This model will be of help to people as a screener who doesn't have easy access to dentists and help flag possible issues using pictures of their oral cavity.
